Assalamu Alaikum!

May the blessings of Allah fill your life with happiness, prosperity, and peace. On this joyous occasion of Eid, may your heart be filled with joy and your home with warmth. May every prayer you make be answered and every dream you hold come true.

A Personal Note

Eid is a time to reflect on the blessings we have been given and to share our joy with those around us. As we celebrate this beautiful occasion together, I pray that Allah strengthens the bonds between us, fills our hearts with gratitude, and grants us the wisdom to walk on the path of righteousness. May this Eid bring new beginnings, fresh hopes, and joyous moments to cherish forever.

Hadith of the Prophet ﷺ

"When the day of Eid al-Fitr arrives, Allah boasts about you to His angels and says: 'O My angels, what is the reward of a worker who has completed his work?' They say: 'Our Lord, his reward is that he be given his payment in full.' He says: 'My servants have fulfilled what I made obligatory upon them, then they came out raising their voices in supplication. I swear by My majesty, My glory, My generosity, and My exalted position, I will surely answer them.' Then He says: 'Return, for I have forgiven you.'"

— Narrated in various collections
